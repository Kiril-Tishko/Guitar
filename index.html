Early 1980s McPherson JL-55M 12-string Acoustic, Serial Number 6!

Today McPherson Guitars are known for their incredible high-end acoustics based out of Wisconsin. However, before that Matt McPherson’s father designed guitars and had them built in Japan. This early production JL-55M is awesome and has a sound that’s quite impossible to find anywhere else! Solid spruce top, rosewood back/sides, unique sound hole layout and built in electronics!

Mathew McPhereson, Sr was granted a patent #4317402 for his incredible invention of a three sound hole design which is responsible for this one of a kind instrument.
